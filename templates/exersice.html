Walking

Start with a daily brisk walk for at least 30 minutes. You can gradually increase your pace and time as you become more comfortable.

Cycling

Riding a bicycle is a low-impact exercise that's great for beginners. It's easy on the joints and helps burn calories.

Bodyweight Exercises

These exercises use your body's weight for resistance. Start with simple exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. Aim for 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that is gentle on the joints. It's excellent for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.
